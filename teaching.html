Teaching materials

Here you will find a small sample of the teaching materials that I have prepared during the last few years. Unless otherwise stated, everything here is released under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike licence.

Fundamentals of linear algebra for Neuroscientists
G. Gomez-Herrero
Course: Advanced Human Neurophysiology, Free University Amsterdam
Latest version (pdf and LaTex sources): http://germangh.com/linear_algebra

Summary:

In this lecture we present some very fundamental concepts of linear algebra that are heavily used in Neuroimaging. Namely, the goal of this lecture is to provide the fundamental substrate neccessary for understanding the common approaches to the EEG and EEG inverse problem.

Exercises for an introductory Digital Signal Processing course
G. Gomez-Herrero
Course: Introduction to Signal Processing, Tampere University of Technology
Latest version (pdf and LaTex sources): http://germangh.com/idsp_exercises

Summary:

Five sets of exercises, four sets of homework assignments and one sample exam. The solutions to most of the sets are also available. Below you can find links to the pdf versions of all documents, but you can also get the Latex sources and raw images.

  • Exercise sets including solutions: ex 1, ex 2, ex 3, ex 3b (optional, more difficult), ex 4, ex 5.
  • Homework sets without solutions: hw 1, hw 2, hw 3.
  • Homework sets including solutions: hw 4.
  • Sample exam including solutions: exam 1.
